HackerEarth Deep Learning challenge: Identify the dance form(click here to see the rank)

The dataset consists of 364 images belonging to 8 categories, namely manipuri, bharatanatyam, odissi, kathakali, kathak, sattriya, kuchipudi, and mohiniyattam. I secured 195 rank and was among the top 5% competitors. I really enjoyed this competition. I used VGG-16 as my model and managed to get around 100% accuracy on train set and 79% accuracy on test set. It could have improved if I would have used different seeding.

HackerEarth Machine Learning challenge: Love is love(click here to see rank)

The dataset consists of quotes that are uploaded during Pride Month. I secured 79th rank in this competition and was among the top 2% competitors. I used pytesseract for OCR but I faced problem in detecting texts from images in the data as the images were of very poor quality. I could have get better accuracy with google vision API.
